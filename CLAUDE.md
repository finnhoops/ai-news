# Editorial Direction

NeuralFeed is designed to give the best news about AI across various sectors for anyone wanting to learn more about artificial intelligence. The website uses all of its resources to completely search the internet for the best and most reliable AI news, and that is what it displays — within the articles and videos that are linked.

- ALL content across every tab is about artificial intelligence
- The sidebar tabs (Finance, Software, Healthcare, etc.) are domain filters — they show AI news that fits that industry, not a shift in topic
- Nothing on this site is AI-generated; all articles and videos come from external publications, researchers, and creators via RSS and curated links
- When adding or changing feeds, every feed must be AI-specific or a publication with strong, consistent AI coverage
- The goal is reliability and breadth: surface the most trustworthy AI news from across the web, organized by the sector it affects
